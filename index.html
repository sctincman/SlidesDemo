Perovskite Solar Cells

Class of minerals with very interesting structure (resulting in many many applications)

Recent and explosive increase in efficiency over the past decade using Pb/methylammonium (Specifically MAPbI3).

Large electron lifetime, diffusion length, and mobility, in addition to large absorptivity, large carrier concentration, ideal bandgap...

  • All available to a solution processable material

Perovskite Challenges

Aside from improving efficiency, there are some interesting problems to investigate

  • Lifetime: short and moisture sensitive
  • Leaching of Pb into environment
  • Replace Pb with Sn: understand performance difference
  • Replace spiro-OMeTAD with something more feasible
